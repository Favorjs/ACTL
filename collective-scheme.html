Collective Investment Schemes

A Collective Investment Scheme is ideal for individuals and institutions looking to grow wealth through diversified, professionally managed investments. It is particularly beneficial for Individual Investors, Businesses & Organizations Retirement & Pension Funds, High-Net-Worth Individuals By pooling resources, investors gain access to broader markets, reduced risk, and professional fund management

At Apel Capital & Trust Limited, we provide trusteeship services for Collective Investment Schemes (CIS), offering investors a secure and efficient way to grow their wealth. Our role ensures that investors' interests are protected, and the scheme operates in compliance with regulatory standards.
