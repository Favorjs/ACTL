Apel Child Education Trust

This is a tailored towards parents or intending parents who have decided to set aside funds in a trust strictly for the education of his/her children or wards (Beneficiaries).

FEATURES


It enables you to have concrete plans towards your child's education. The fund is separate from your personal estate and is therefore inaccessible to Probate or Creditors. High return on investment. Fund Management by qualified professionals.

BENEFITS


  • In the event of eventualities, the Trust will take over payment of the child/ward’s fees.
  • The fund is separate from Client’s personal estate and is therefore inaccessible to Probate or Creditors.
  • Any excess in the Client’s account after meeting the objective shall be transferred to a named beneficiary.
